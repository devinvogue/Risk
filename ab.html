GENESIS

Blood group AB

WHO TO DONATE AND RECEIVE BLOOD FROM

There are two sides of the AB blood type. The AB+ and AB-. The AB+ can donate blood to only AB+ And receive blood from all other blood type The AB- can also donate blood to AB-, AB+ And receive blood from AB-, A-, B- and O- blood type
