GENESIS

Blood group A

WHO TO DONATE AND RECEIVE BLOOD FROM

There are two sides of the A blood type. The A+ and A-. The A+ can donate blood to: A+, AB+ And receive blood from A-, A+, O+,O- The A- can also donate blood to A-, A+, AB-, AB+ And receive blood from A- and O- blood type

BEST WORKOUTS (EXERCISE)

Intense exercises leads to fatigue and stiffness. You should opt for calming activities that helps you focus and protect your joints like Pilates, yoga, Tai Chi and Isometric exercises.

BEST DIET

A meat-free diet based on fruits and vegetables, beans and legumes, and whole grains is the ideal diet for you. You are a natural vegetarian To lose weight, people with group A are to follow a vegetarian diet.

DISEASES

You are highly at the risk of getting stressed out and anxious. You are at a higher risk of developing type 2 Diabetes and stomach cancer and you are very prone to malaria You have a lower risk of developing Coronary heart disease compared to blood group B and AB
